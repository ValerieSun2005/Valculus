6.4 Exercises — Integration by Partial Fractions

Click on a problem number to see its solution.

For each of exercises 1–6, perform the partial fraction decomposition.

\(1.\) \(\ds \frac{2}{(x + 3)(x - 2)}\) \(2.\) \(\ds \frac{3x - 1}{x^2 - 8x - 9}\) \(3.\) \(\ds \frac{2x + 3}{9x^2 - x} \)
\(4.\) \(\ds \frac{t^2}{8t^3 - 1}\) \(5.\) \(\ds \frac{x^3 + 1}{9 - x^2}\) \(6.\) \(\ds \frac{3 - 10x^3}{5x^3 + 8x^2 - 11x} \)

For each of exercises 7–17, evaluate the integral.

\(7.\) \(\ds \int \frac{4 - 3x}{(7x + 3)(4 - x)} \, \dd x\) \(8.\) \(\ds \int \frac{x}{2x^2 - 5x - 3} \, \dd x\) \(9.\) \(\ds \int \frac{3t^2 + 2}{5t^2 - 25t} \, \dd t \)

\(13.\) \(\ds \int \frac{9 + 5q^2}{q^3 - q^2 - 12q} \, \dd q\) \(14.\) \(\ds \int \frac{x^4 + 1}{16x - 4x^3} \, \dd x\) \(15.\) \(\ds \int \frac{x}{(2x + 1)(x^2 + 4x + 1)} \, \dd x\)
\(16.\) \(\ds \int \frac{7w^3 - 9}{(w + 2)(4 - w^2)} \, \dd w \) \(17.\) \(\ds \int \frac{5}{2x^3 + 3x} \, \dd x\)
