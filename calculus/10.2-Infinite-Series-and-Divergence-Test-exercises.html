If an object is dropped from a height \(H\) and rebounds to a new height \(h,\) then its coefficient of restitution is \[C_R = \sqrt{\frac{h}{H}} \pd\] A basketball is dropped from a height of \(7\) feet and rebounds to a height of \(5\) feet.
  1. Calculate \(C_R\) for the trial.
  2. The ball continues to rebound under the same coefficient of restitution. What is the total vertical distance traveled by the ball?
\(50.\)

How much money must the government spend to generate \(\$60\) billion of economic activity?
\(51.\)
When banks provide loans, a country's money supply increases. Banks loan money to other banks, but banks are required to hold a certain proportion—called the reserve ratio \((R)\)—of their total assets. Suppose a deposit of \(x\) dollars is made to a bank account. To maximize its revenue, the bank holds on to \(xR\) dollars and lends out \(x(1 - R)\) dollars to another bank. The new bank then lends out \(x(1 - R)^2\) dollars to a third bank, and this repetition leads to rapid multiplication of the initial deposit of \(x\) dollars.
  1. Based on an infinite series, derive an expression for \(M,\) the increase in the money supply when an initial deposit of \(x\) dollars is made in a country whose reserve ratio is \(R.\)
  2. During times of inflation or rapid growth, the reserve ratio is often increased because a growing money supply accelerates inflation. In the early \(1980\)s, amid a period of stagflation (inflation with contracting productivity), the Federal Reserve (the Fed) hiked the reserve ratio to \(10 \%.\) Determine, in terms of \(x\) and \(R,\) how much less money is added to the money supply when \(R\) is increased by \(0.1.\)
  3. The reserve ratio is often decreased during times of recession to encourage economic growth. In 2020, amid the onset of quarantine and lockdowns due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the Fed dropped the reserve ratio to \(0.\) Calculate and interpret \(\lim_{R \to 0^+} M.\)

  2. If \(\sum_{n = 1}^\infty a_n\) diverges, then prove that \(c\sum_{n = 1}^\infty a_n\) also diverges.
