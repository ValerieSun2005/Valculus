\(37.\)

\(38.\)
Let \(F(x) = 4 \sin 8x \cos 2x\) be the force, in pounds, exerted upon an object located at a position of \(x\) feet. How much work is required to move an object from \(x = 0\) to \(x = \pi/4 \ques\)
\(39.\)

\(40.\)
